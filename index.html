Student Login

Login with your UID and Password to access your Student Services and Account. The CUIMS will keep a record of your progress and keep you apprised of the latest updates.

LMS Login

Staff Login

Login using your Employee Code and Password to access your account, keep track of your progress and other official services.

Admission Login

For Admission Staff only. Use your Employee Code and Password to log in for admission related services.
